About us

The geographical position of the lands of Kantemir-Agro LLP, located in the north of Kazakhstan, gives this farm a number of advantages. Being a part of the West Siberian Lowland, these lands have a high soil quality index, which in some places reaches 70-75 points. The chernozems of the forest-steppe zone, on which the Partnership performs the entire technological process, guarantee the receipt of environmentally friendly crop products. The Partnership is engaged in the production of spring and winter wheat, barley, oats, peas, soybeans, rapeseed and flax.
The Partnership mainly uses traditional technologies, but taking into account the fact that the climate of this zone is sharply continental, we sometimes work using zero-till technology. The Partnership is deliberately moving towards the creation of an agricultural cluster i.e. from the production of wheat and flour to baking bakery products and selling them to the population.

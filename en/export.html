    Delivery Basis

    EXW Nikolaevka — ex-farm price. DAP border — delivery to the Chinese border inclusive.

  • 🚂

    Logistics

    Rail transport in hopper wagons. Loading stations: Ishim, Petropavlovsk. Bulk or PP bags 50 kg.

  • 💳

    Payment Terms

    Letter of credit (L/C) — preferred for new buyers. 100% prepayment. Flexible terms for established partners.

  • 📄

    Documentation

    Phytosanitary certificate (RK), Certificate of Origin CT-1, customs declaration, CMR / Rail waybill, invoice, packing list.

  • 🔍

    Quality Control

    Independent SGS or Bureau Veritas inspection available on request at buyer's cost. Quality certificates — standard.

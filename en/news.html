News

Field work, harvest results, company developments

March 2025

Preparing for Spring Fieldwork 2025

All machinery has undergone full maintenance. Premium wheat variety seeds have been purchased. Machine operators are ready to enter the field when weather conditions allow. This year we plan to expand flaxseed and rapeseed areas.

2024

New Crop Introduced — Soybean (50 ha)

In 2024 we sowed soybean for the first time on 50 hectares. It is a high-demand crop on international markets. First-year results are encouraging. We plan to expand the planting area in 2025.

2024

New Grain Warehouses Completed

Construction of modern grain storage facilities has been completed. Increased storage capacity allows for more flexible supply management and selling grain at better prices.

Autumn 2024

2024 Yield — 23 c/ha

Average yield across all crops in the 2024 season reached 23 centners per hectare. This result was achieved through precision agriculture techniques and high-quality seeds.

2023

Red Lentils Added to Portfolio

In 2023 the company began growing red lentils. The crop showed strong results and enjoys stable demand in the export markets of the Middle East and South Asia.

December 2024

20 Years in the Market!

In December 2024 Kantemir Agro LLP celebrated its 20th anniversary. Over this time the company grew from a small farm into a leading grain exporter in North Kazakhstan Oblast.

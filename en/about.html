About the Company

21 years of experience, 7,000 hectares, 41–50 employees

History

Founded in 2004

Kantemir Agro LLP was registered on 27 December 2004 in Nikolaevka village, Yesil district, North Kazakhstan Oblast. Over 21 years the company has grown into one of the region's leading agricultural enterprises.

We cultivate 7,000 hectares of arable land, grow 6 crops, and export to overseas markets. Our fleet of approximately 50 machines ranges from K-700 tractors to modern foreign-made equipment.

The company has been a VAT payer since 2005. Zero tax violations, zero lawsuits, full compliance with the State Register of Kazakhstan.
